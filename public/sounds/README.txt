Place ambient sound loops here. All files should be MP3.

Required files:
  rain.mp3      — looping rain ambience
  wind.mp3      — looping wind ambience
  crickets.mp3  — looping night insect sounds
  thunder.mp3   — single thunder rumble (one-shot, not looped)

Good free sources (search freesound.org, filter by CC0 license):
  rain:     "rain loop" or "rain ambience loop"
  wind:     "wind loop" or "forest wind ambience"
  crickets: "crickets loop" or "night insects loop"
  thunder:  "thunder crack" or "thunder rumble"

Tips:
  - Keep loops 30–60s long so they don't feel repetitive
  - Normalize all files to a similar loudness level
  - rain.mp3 and wind.mp3 will be crossfaded together during storms
